## Disabling Current Page Links:

~~~css
 a {
       pointer-events: none;
       cursor: default;
}
~~~

## Indenting block of text

<pre>
<div style="text-indent: 1em;">
your text here
</div>
</pre>

## indent an entire block:

<pre>
<div style="margin-left: 1em;">
your text here
</div>
</pre>
